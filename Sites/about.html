From Passion to Profession

My name is Mathilde Willems, a Graphic Design apprentice in my second year at EFP, a program combining studies with practical training. I attend classes twice a week and intern three days a week to gain real-world skills.

Since childhood, I’ve been captivated by visual aesthetics—sketching outfits, exploring color harmony, and coordinating styles. While fashion remains a strong interest, my passion extends to creating designs that blend structure, color, and communication to deliver impactful messages.
