Q&A

Why did you choose to study at EFP?

I chose to study at EFP because I appreciate the balance between theory and practice.

At school, we focus on mastering the fundamentals while also working on diverse projects. Through the internship program, I gain valuable hands-on experience, learning directly from the workplace environment, my internship supervisor, coworkers, and real-world projects.

How long are your studies?

I am pursuing a two-year Graphic Design program, which I will complete in September 2025.

How long went you to Canada?

I stayed in Canada for 8 months. During that time, I studied customer service, improved my English skills, explored a new lifestyle, gained work experience, and immersed myself in many incredible opportunities. It was truly a life-changing experience.

Why did you choose to study Graphic Design?

As mentioned in my personal introduction, I have a passion for visual beauty, organization, creativity, and continuous learning. In my opinion, these qualities are essential for a successful career in graphic design, making it the perfect field for me.
