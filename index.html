Creating a Perfect Grid

Grids are the backbone of clean, organized editorial layouts. They help structure content and guide the reader’s eye.

1) How to Create a Grid:

Choose Columns:
A 12-column grid offers flexibility for various layouts.

2) Set Margins & Gutters:

Define space around and between content for clarity.

3) Use a Baseline Grid:

Align text for consistency and rhythm.

4) Create Modular Layouts:

Organize content into blocks for easy arrangement.

Grids bring balance and readability to your designs, making them visually appealing and easy to navigate.
